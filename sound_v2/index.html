Pressing "Q" once will start: Feather by Nujabes | Pressing "W" once will start: luv (sic) pt3 by Nujabes | Pressing "E" once will start: Reflection Eternal by Nujabes | Pressing "R" once will start: Song of Four Seasons by Nujabes | Pressing a song key twice will restart the song

"Down" will set volume to 0% | "Left" will set volume to 33% | "Up" will set volume to 66% | "Right" will set volume to 99%
